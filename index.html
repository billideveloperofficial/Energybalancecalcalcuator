Daily Energy

Sedentary

Sedentary (Little or no exercise): You do not engage in regular exercise or physical activity for your job. Most of your day is likely spent sitting, with activities like working at a desk job, reading, watching television or using a computer.

 

Light Active

Lightly Active (Light exercise/sports 1-3 days/week): You engage in light physical activity or exercise a few times a week. This could involve taking a walk, participating in a yoga class or having a job that requires some degree of physical activity, like teaching.

 

Mod Active

Moderately Active (Moderate exercise/sports 3-5 days/week): You engage in moderate physical activity or exercise most days of the week. This could be a job that requires physical labor, or consistent exercise like jogging, cycling or weightlifting.

 

Very Active

Very Active (Hard exercise/sports 6-7 days a week): You engage in hard physical activity or strenuous exercise nearly every day. This could include having a physically demanding job like construction, farming or being engaged in intense workouts or sports training on a daily basis.

Extreme Active

Extra Active (Very hard exercise/physical job & exercise 2x/day): This level of activity is common for athletes in training, individuals with physically demanding jobs who also exercise intensely, or those who engage in lengthy, vigorous exercise twice a day.
